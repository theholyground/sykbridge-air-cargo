    Frequently asked questions

    Customs holds are routine for international air freight and usually involve document review or, occasionally, duties owed on the contents. Most clear within 1–2 business days. Your tracking page will update automatically once the shipment is released.

    Some shipments can only be handed to the named recipient, who must sign at the door. If they're unavailable, the driver will leave a redelivery notice with instructions for rescheduling or pickup.

    Address changes are possible before a shipment reaches its destination hub. Contact support with your waybill number and the new address, and we'll confirm whether the change can still be made.

    Scans happen at each hub, so gaps of a day or so during transit are normal, especially over long-haul legs. If it's been more than 3 business days with no update, reach out and we'll look into it directly.

    Contact support within 7 days of delivery with your waybill number and photos of the damage if applicable. We'll open a claim and walk you through next steps.
